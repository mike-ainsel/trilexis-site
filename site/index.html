Bring your own dictionaries

Seven formats, one library — import the files you already have:

  • StarDict .ifo .dict .idx .syn — the most widespread open-source format.
  • MDict .mdx .mdd — the largest community-built library, popular across Asia.
  • DSL (ABBYY Lingvo) .dsl .dsl.dz — the standard across Russia and Eastern Europe.
  • Babylon .bgl — classic Babylon glossaries.
  • ZIM .zim — offline Wikipedia & Kiwix archives.
  • CC-CEDICT .u8 — the open Chinese–English dictionary.
  • CSV / TSV .csv .tsv — your own word lists.
